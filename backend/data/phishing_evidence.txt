NORTHGATE FINANCIAL - SECURITY OPERATIONS CENTER (SOC)
Threat Intelligence & Incident Note / CONFIDENTIAL
Ref: SOC-2026-0619 | Subject: Credential phishing affecting Finance

Timeline of relevant findings:

2026-06-19  A targeted phishing email was delivered to several Finance staff,
            spoofing the internal IT Service Desk ("Action required: password
            re-validation"). The landing page was a pixel-perfect clone of the
            Northgate SSO login.

2026-06-19  User rsharma (Rahul Sharma) entered corporate credentials into the
            cloned page at 14:22. Endpoint telemetry confirms the POST to the
            attacker-controlled host. Credentials (username + password) were
            harvested. Rahul Sharma was NOT aware the page was fraudulent.

2026-06-20  Password reset was recommended to the user but the ticket stalled;
            multi-factor was NOT enforced on the legacy VPN gateway at the time.

Threat-intel enrichment:
  - The phishing kit exfiltrated harvested credentials to command-and-control
    infrastructure previously attributed to an external threat actor.
  - Associated indicator of compromise (IOC): source IP 41.220.13.7
    (geolocated abroad, "Lagos, NG" egress; part of a known credential-abuse
    cluster). This IP has been used to replay stolen corporate logins.

Analyst note: any successful login for rsharma originating from 41.220.13.7
should be treated as ACCOUNT TAKEOVER by the external actor, not as legitimate
activity by the employee. The stolen credentials remained valid because the
account password had not yet been rotated.
